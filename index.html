Why we built TeamStation AI

  • Hiring in LATAM was fragmented: vendors, recruiters, spreadsheets, and bias-prone interviews.
  • Onboarding stalled: unmanaged devices, slow access, compliance gaps, and week-one idle time.
  • Performance was fuzzy: opinions over observable behaviors; no shared definitions for “good.”

We unified the stack into an Intelligent Services Infrastructure and paired it with a Cognitive AI Operating System so teams start productive, secure, and measurable from day one.

What the Nearshore IT Co-Pilot™ does

  • Hire, equip, secure, and pay LATAM engineers — under one SLA.
  • Devices + MDM (Mobile Device Management), baseline security, identity, access, and compliance.
  • Payroll/EOR, benefits, and optional offices — all integrated with onboarding flows.
  • Process you can trust: Demo → Platform Onboarding → Post Jobs & Calibration → Interview & Evaluation → Hire & Day-One Onboarding → Performance (BARS) & Growth.

Axiom Cortex™ — Cognitive AI Operating System

  • 44 psychometric + NLP signals for alignment, analysis, and synthesis of engineering evidence.
  • Method: semantic chunking in RAG and staged/multi-step prompting with language-fairness calibration.
  • Expert review of flags before roll-up; mapped to BARS (ratings tied to observable behaviors).
  • L2-aware scoring separates content signal from delivery noise, so ideas are judged fairly.
